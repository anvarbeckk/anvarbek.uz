About Me

Hi! I'm Anvarbek Jumamuradov, a 15-year-old junior backend developer from Uzbekistan. My programming journey began at 13, and I've been on a quest to master backend development since then.

My coding adventure started with HTML and CSS tutorials, forming the basis of my passion for web development. Within a year, I delved into Python, fascinated by its versatility and power.

Recognizing the importance of hands-on experience, I sought internships in the open-source community, collaborating with seasoned developers. These experiences not only sharpened my coding skills but also instilled in me the ethos of community-driven development.

Ever enthusiastic about staying ahead in the tech curve, I am currently immersing myself in the world of Go (Golang). This new venture is a testament to my commitment to continuous learning, ensuring that my skill set remains at the forefront of the ever-evolving backend development landscape.
